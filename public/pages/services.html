How Carma works

Find and reserve your perfect car

Whatever make or model, find and reserve your perfect car from our full range of high quality pre-loved cars.

Payments, financing, trade in. We're here to help.

We know everyone's car buying experience is a little different. Financing, buying outright, trading in - we're here to help make the process smooth and enjoyable.

Choose free delivery or collect, then start driving

Once you're ready, you can choose to have your car delivered for free to your door, or collect it from our convenient location in Alexandria, Sydney.

Pay your way with Carma’s flexible payment options

Spread the cost with finance

Get a great rate and flexible finance options on the car you really want. Working with trusted, carefully selected lenders, we take the hassle out of negotiating a good deal. Get a quote and apply in under 20 minutes.

Finance through our select panel of lenders, and we’ll provide a dedicated Carma finance consultant to help you through the whole application and approval process.

Pay in full for faster delivery

Choose to pay in full by credit card or bank transfer for the quickest way to get your new Carma car. We accept most major Australian credit and debit cards.

Bring your own financier
